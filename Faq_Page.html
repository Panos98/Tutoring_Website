Web Tutor FAQ

How do I get a tutor?

By signing into the website we will send you and email with a couple of questions. These questions pertain to youracademic needs, grade level and the times you are available to be tutored. We will then take your answers to these questions and pair you up with the best matched tutor that we have available.

Who are the tutors?

The tutors are predominately grade 12 IB and Academic students at STA. This students have proven academic success and have had experience tutoring in the past.

Which subjects can I be tutored in?

At the moment we are focusing out tutoring on mathematics. This entails grade 9/10 general math and specialized tutoring for algebra, calculus and advances functions.

How many times a week do you recommend for tutoring?

We recommend 1 day a week for tutoring. Our tutors are pretty flexible so try to plan your tutoring sessions around the tests you have in class. We recommend a tutoring sessions 2 days before every class so that you can ask questions and prepare well before test day!
